PHP workshop with OALUG

For our January meeting, we will be co-hosting a beginner to immediate workshop on PHP with the Ogden Area Linux Users Group. Please note that the date, time, and location will be different than usual. The workshop will be held on the Weber State University Ogden Campus on Saturday, 27 January, from ten until fourteen hundred hours. Those interested in attending should RSVP to both the OALUG mailing list and the UPHPU Administrator. Please include your name as name badges are being pre-made.

We are also still seeking an additional volunteer to present and ask that anyone willing to participate in running the workshop contact the UPHPU Administrator with your name and availability.

  • Date: Saturday, 27 January 2007
  • Time: 10:00 - 14:00pm MST
  • Location: Weber State University Ogden Campus
  • Building: Technical Education Building north lab (still tentative)
  • Map: launch
  • Directions: Turn North on Dixon Drive from Harrison Blvd. Turn right on Edvalson going East. Edvalson makes a slight bend left. In the bend, turn right into the A2 parking lot. The Technical Education Building is identified on the URL above as "22 TE".
